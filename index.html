About

I am Ariel Casia based in Singapore. I'm in Interior Design and has worked with top hospitality firms like HBA, EDG, Wilson Associates, and Smallwoods. In 2018 I switched to digital sketching. I have many doubts at first. Is it scaled? Is it easy to learn? Can I use in my real work process? Will the output still look like my manual sketch? In a day, I answered yes to all these. I know what your thinking, another App with so many tools to learn and might just slow your process. I promise, this is the most simplest drawing app you'll learn and start to use in less than an hour...

I'll teach you an App called Arrette. The best App to sketch in scale. It only has few basic tools like the pen, select, copy, paste, rotate, flip. This is an easy, effective remote work skill to learn and make you more efficient, fast and agile.

These are real work sketches I drew using Arrette App. Click image to enlarge, scroll-up to zoom-in, scroll-down to zoom-out, press button and hold to pan and click image to exit.

This App version has some disabled tools compared to the paid version but you can still draw all the sketches shown above. Try it and get a feel.
